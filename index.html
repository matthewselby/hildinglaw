Paul Hilding is a retired attorney. For most of his thirty five year legal career, he practiced in the field of insurance coverage and bad faith law, representing policy holders exclusively. He now testifies on issues related to his former legal practice.

Mr. Hilding's educational background, employment history and professional achievements are as follows:

  • Education: Duke Law School, 1983; Ohio Wesleyan 1980 (Magna Cum Laude; Phi Beta Kappa).
  • Employment: Founding Partner, Hilding Law Firm, 2006-2018; Founding Partner, Hilding Kipnis Lyon & Kelly, 1993-2006; Associate and Partner, Brobeck, Phleger & Harrison, 1987-1993; Associate, Higgs Fletcher & Mack, 1983-1986.
